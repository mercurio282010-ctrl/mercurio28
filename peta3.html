LUZON:

Baguio City
LION'S HEAD'

A cool mountain city in Benguet. Famous for Burnham Park, strawberries, and pine forests. It’s the perfect break from Manila’s heat.

Albay
Mayon Volcano

Known for its perfect cone shape. One of the most active but most beautiful volcanoes in the world. Best seen from Cagsawa Ruins.
Puerto Princesa
Underground River

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Palawan. It has an 8.2km river inside a cave with amazing rock formations and bats.
Vigan City
Heritage Town

A Spanish colonial town in Ilocos Sur. Walk on cobblestone streets and ride a kalesa along Calle Crisologo.
Ifugao
Banaue Rice Terraces

Called the “8th Wonder of the World.” Built by Ifugao ancestors 2,000 years ago by hand. It looks like giant steps on the mountains.
VISAYAS:

Boracay
White Beach

A 4km beach in Aklan with powdery white sand. One of the world’s best beaches. Great for swimming, sunset views, and island hopping.
Bohol
Chocolate Hills

Over 1,200 cone-shaped hills that turn brown in summer. A unique natural landmark in Carmen, Bohol with a great viewing deck.
Cebu
Kawasan Falls

A 3-tiered waterfall with bright blue water. Popular for canyoneering, cliff jumping, and bamboo raft rides in Badian, Cebu.
Siargao
Daku Island

A tropical island near Siargao. Famous for white sand, clear water, and “Cloud 9” surf waves. Perfect for beach hopping and surfing.
Occidental Mindoro
Apo Reef

The largest coral reef system in the Philippines. A top diving spot with turtles, sharks, and colorful corals. Protected marine park.
MINDANAO:

Davao City
Mount Apo

At 2,954m, it’s the highest mountain in the Philippines. Located between Davao and Cotabato. Great for hiking and nature trails.
Camiguin Island
Island Born of Fire

A small island with 7 volcanoes. Famous for the Sunken Cemetery, White Island, and hot springs. Also known for sweet lanzones.
Mati, Davao Oriental
Dahican Beach

A 7km long white sand beach. Called the “Surfing Capital of Mindanao.” Good for skimboarding, camping, and horseback riding.
Surigao del Sur
Enchanted River

A deep blue river with crystal-clear water in Hinatuan. No one knows where it comes from. Has a fish sanctuary with daily feeding.
South Cotabato
Lake Sebu

Home of the T’boli tribe. Known for tilapia, 7 waterfalls, and T’nalak weaving. You can also try the zipline over the lake.
